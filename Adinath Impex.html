About Adinath Impex

Your Reliable Partner Since 2008

Adinath Impex is a leading importer and trader of premium plastic raw materials based in Mumbai, India. With over 17 years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the plastic raw materials market.

We specialize in importing high-quality plastic raw materials from international markets and distributing them across India. Our extensive network and industry expertise enable us to provide our clients with the best products at competitive prices.
